Type of Staff Change:
New Staff :
A new staff member is someone new to the Diocese of Lincoln and has never worked for {{ userContext.curSchoolName }} or any other Diocese of Lincoln Schools.
Example 1: A teacher coming to teach at {{ userContext.curSchoolName }} from out of state.
Example 2: A teacher who will be teaching for the first time after just completing college.
Example 3: A teacher who previously taught at the public schools but is teaching for the first time at {{ userContext.curSchoolName }}.
Transferring-In Staff :
A transferring-in staff member is someone coming INTO {{ userContext.curSchoolName }} from another Diocese of Lincoln School. This can also include staff members who work across multiple Diocese of Lincoln schools, including {{ userContext.curSchoolName }}.
Example 1: A teacher coming INTO {{ userContext.curSchoolName }} from any other Diocese of Lincoln school.
Example 2: A priest or religious sister who previously taught at another Diocese of Lincoln School and is being reassigned to {{ userContext.curSchoolName }}.
Example 3: A teacher who previously taught at {{ userContext.curSchoolName }} and currently needs to be reactivated because they are returning.
Example 4: A band teacher who teaches at both {{ userContext.curSchoolName }} and other Diocese of Lincoln Schools.
Position/Job Change :
This option is for someone who is currently a staff member at {{ userContext.curSchoolName }} but is going to change positions or jobs at {{ userContext.curSchoolName }}.
Example 1: A teacher at {{ userContext.curSchoolName }} who used to teach first grade but will now teach second grade.
Example 2: A teacher at {{ userContext.curSchoolName }} who used to teach computers but will now be the administrative assistant in the front office.
Substitute:
A Substitute is a staff member that fills in for a regular teacher, managing classroom instruction and activities during their absence. There are three distinctions according to the Substitute Teacher Policy for Technology Access
Short Term Sub (STS) : A Substitute that comes in for a single day or two at a time.You do not need to submit a Staff Change for STS
Frequent Short Term Sub (FSTS) : A Substitute that frequently subs in our school system more than 5 times per year total in any of our Catholic schools.
Long Term Sub (LTS) : A Substitute covering for a teacher on a replacement basis for an extended period of time. The distinguishing factor will be the need for the sub to have access to the gradebook in PowerSchool.
Name Change :
This option is for someone who had their name legally changed and needs it updated in our systems.
Example: A staff member who got married and needs their title and last name changed.
Exiting Staff :
An exiting staff member is somone who is not returning to {{ userContext.curSchoolName }}.
Use the Exiting Staff form even if the staff member is leaving {{ userContext.curSchoolName }} for another Diocese of Lincoln School.
Example 1: A teacher who will not be returning to {{ userContext.curSchoolName }} next school year.
Example 2: A staff member who quits unexpectedly and their account needs to be deactivated.
