{{userContext.pageStatus}} Staff Change

New Staff :
A new staff member is someone new to the Diocese of Lincoln and has never worked for ~(schoolname) or any other Diocese of Lincoln Schools.
For Example: A teacher coming to teach at ~(schoolname) from out of state.
Another Example: A teacher who will be teaching for the first time after just completing college.
Another Example: A teacher who previously taught at the public schools but is teaching for the first time at ~(schoolname).
Transferring Staff :
A transferring staff member is someone coming INTO ~(schoolname) from another Diocese of Lincoln School.
For Example: A teacher coming INTO ~(schoolname) from any other Diocese of Lincoln school.
Another Example: A priest or religious sister who previously taught at another Diocese of Lincoln School and is being reassigned to ~(schoolname).
Position/Job Change :
Choose this option for someone who is going to still be a staff member at ~(schoolname) but is going to change positions or jobs within ~(schoolname).
For Example: A teacher at ~(schoolname) used to teach first grade but now is going to teach second grade.
Another Example: A teacher at ~(schoolname) used to teach computers but is now going to be the administrative assistant in the front office.
Name Change :
Choose this option for someone who needs to, or will need to in the near future, legally change their name.
Example: A staff member who is going to get married and needs their last name changed.
Exiting Staff :
A staff member not returning to ~(schoolname).
Example: A teacher who will not be returning to ~(schoolname) next school year.
Another Example: A staff member who quits unexpectedly and their account needs to be deactivated.
